Pavel Durov

The man who fights for our internet freedom

Timeline of Pavel's life:

  • 1984 - Born in Leningrad, USSR
  • 2001 - Graduated from the Academic gymnasium with honors
  • 2006 - Graduated from SPBU and created Vkontakte, also known as VK
  • 2007 - Vkontakte becomes 3rd most popular site in Russia, Pavel as a VK CEO was was recognized as one of the winners in the contest "Best young entrepreneurs of 2007"
  • 2008 - Number of users VK exceeded up to 20 million
  • 2011 - Pavel, with a fortune of 7.9 billion rubles, ranked 350th in the ranking of Russian billionaires
  • 2012 - Donated 1 million dollars to Wikipedia, site "VK" becomes 26th most popular in the world
  • 2013 - In August 24th first client of new Pavel's project Telegram was presented
  • 2013 - FSB start pressure on VK team, they wanted personal information of people from Euromaidan groups from Ukraine. Pavel rejected to provide any inforamtion and said that this is illegal and crime
  • 2014 - Sold all his remaining VK actions to his friend Ivan Tavrin. Pavel said "What you own, sooner or later begins to own you."
  • 2014 - Leaves Russia, received citizenship of the island state Federation of Saint Kitts and Nevis. Pavel said: "I'm afraid there's no going back. Especially after I publicly refused to cooperate with the authorities."
  • 2016 - New project Telegram reached more than 100 million users and delivers about 15 billion messages daily
  • 2017 - FSB start pressure on Telegram, they wanted access to users data and encryption keys. Pavel rejected.
  • 2018 - Telegram was banned in Russia by Roskomnadzor. However Pavel said that Telegram still will be online and will work in Russia. Pavel said: "Threats to block Telegram if we do not give out users private data will not lead to anything. Telegram protects your freedom and privacy."
  • 2018 - Pavel was awarded the prize of the Union of journalists of Kazakhstan for the principled position against censorship and state interference in the free correspondence of citizens on the Internet
  • 2019 - Pavel works hard to improve and keep telegram independent. To be continued...
